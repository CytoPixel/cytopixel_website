About

Built by scientists, for scientists

CytoPixel Software was founded by Arjun Raj at the University of Pennsylvania. After years of building custom analysis tools in academic labs, we decided the broader research community shouldn't have to do the same.

We believe research software should be open source, cloud-accessible, and designed around real scientific workflows. No forced vendor lock-in. No black-box algorithms. Just tools that help you see what's happening in your data and make better decisions.
