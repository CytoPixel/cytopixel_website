About

Built by scientists, for scientists

CytoPixel Software was founded by Arjun Raj at the University of Pennsylvania. We believe research software should be open source, cloud-accessible, and designed around real scientific workflows. Our mission is to make research tools accessible and delightful — no forced vendor lock-in, no black-box algorithms.
